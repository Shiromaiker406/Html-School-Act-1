The Solar System

The solar system is a fascinating system comprising the Sun, planets, moons,asteroids, comets, and other celestial objects. Its study provides insights into the formation and evolution of celestial bodies.

Learn more about the solar system

Key components of the Solar System

  • The Sun: The star at the center, providing light, heat, and energy to sustain life on Earth.
  • Moons: Natural satellites that orbit planets, varying in size and composition.
  • Asteroids: Small rocky bodies mainly located in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter.
  • Comets: Icy bodies with bright comas and tails when near the Sun.

Chemical Reactions

Chemical reactions are processes that transform substances into new ones. They play a crucial role in natural phenomena and human activities.

Examples of Chemical Reactions

  • Combustion: The reaction of a substance with oxygen, often producing heat and light. Example: Burning of wood (C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂ → 6CO₂ + 6H₂O + Energy).
  • Photosynthesis: The process by which green plants and some other organisms use sunlight to synthesize foods with the help of chlorophyll. Example: Conversion of carbon dioxide and water into glucose and oxygen (6CO₂ + 6H₂O + light energy → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂).
  • Rust Formation: The oxidation of iron in the presence of oxygen and water, resulting in the formation of iron oxide (rust). Example: Rusting of iron objects (4Fe + 3O₂ + 6H₂O → 4Fe(OH)₃).
  • Neutralization: The reaction between an acid and a base to form water and a salt. Example: Reaction between hydrochloric acid and sodium hydroxide (HCl + NaOH → NaCl + H₂O).
  • Synthesis: The combination of two or more substances to form a new compound. Example: Synthesis of water from hydrogen and oxygen gases (2H₂ + O₂ → 2H₂O).
  • Decomposition: A chemical reaction where a compound breaks down into simpler substances. Example: Decomposition of hydrogen peroxide into water and oxygen (2H₂O₂ → 2H₂O + O₂).
  • Redox Reaction: A reaction involving the transfer of electrons between reactants. Example: Oxidation of iron metal by oxygen to form rust (4Fe + 3O₂ → 2Fe₂O₃).
